Emblemicons is an open-source library of most commonly and frequetly used icons in product development, product design, business presentations, and academic projects. You are completely free to use and dowload the icons for your personal and business requirements.

With Emblemicons, you can choose from a pool of 1000+ frequently used icons and do the following 4 things:
  1. You can download PNG and JPG images in 6 available sizes (12px, 18px, 24px, 36px, 48px, 72px).
  2. You can download SVG file and style it with CSS.
  3. You can download PDF file too.
  4. You can copy the HTML code and use in your projects directly.
